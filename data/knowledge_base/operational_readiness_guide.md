# Operational Readiness Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the operational readiness principles used by OpsGuardian AI.

Operational readiness is evaluated through three dimensions:

1. Workforce Skills
2. Operational Capacity
3. Incident Response Preparedness

## Workforce Skills

Critical operational roles should maintain certification coverage above 75%.

Recommended certifications:

### Cloud Engineer
- AZ-900
- AZ-204

### DevOps Engineer
- AZ-900
- AZ-400

### Site Reliability Engineer (SRE)
- AZ-900
- AZ-104
- AZ-400

### Data Engineer
- DP-900
- DP-203

## Capacity Thresholds

Healthy:
- Average workload below 35h

Moderate:
- Between 35h and 42h

High:
- Above 42h

## Operational Readiness Score

Readiness should combine:

- Skills Coverage
- Incident Preparedness
- Workforce Capacity

Scores:

80-100 = Low Risk
60-79 = Medium Risk
0-59 = High Risk
