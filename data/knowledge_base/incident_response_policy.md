# Incident Response Policy

## Objective

Provide guidance for evaluating incident response readiness.

## Readiness Factors

### Team Structure

Recommended:

- Cloud Engineer
- DevOps Engineer
- Site Reliability Engineer

### Technical Coverage

Recommended certifications:

- AZ-204
- AZ-400
- AZ-104

### Simulation Principles

Incident simulations should evaluate:

- Team composition
- Certification coverage
- Operational workload
- Escalation capability

## Risk Indicators

High Risk:

- Missing critical roles
- Certification coverage below 60%
- Workload above 42h

Medium Risk:

- Partial certification coverage
- Moderate workload

Low Risk:

- Full role coverage
- Strong certification coverage
- Healthy workload
